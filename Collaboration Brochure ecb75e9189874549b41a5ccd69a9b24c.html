About Us

Hunters Clan was born out of the idle minds of a group of young graduates planning to revolutionise online learning and content publishing more interactive and engaging.

What do we do?

We conduct online/offline treasure hunts with 2 distinct objectives

  1. Learning through Quizzing : We create content based on client requirements so that the addressing crowd could be taught the desired content more actively in a fun way.
  1. Event Quizzing : Tailor made contents are created to ensure the client enjoys better audience reach and brand awareness.

How do we do?

Ordinary online treasure hunts are based on PDF encryption. It is flawed as it is not strong enough to withstand cheaters. Here, we are doing it by a CTFd based web platform, hosted online and built with automatic brute force protection to keep out cheaters.

What is CTFd?

It is an open source Capture The Flag (CTF) framework for hackers. It is basically a super secure framework to build a website that can be used to host CTF events.

The Game Design

Treasure hunt games are series of challenges that gets unlocked one after other, when solved. These challenges are puzzles that are designed according to the theme of the event. These challenges are designed in way to think out of the box and these challenges when solved gives immense pleasure out of satisfaction.

We do this in a user-friendly interface with real time statistics to bring the competitive mind in everyone to action.

Game Play

Step 1: Game will be hosted at www.huntersclan.games.

Step 2: Players can login 1hr before the game launch with the credentials provided and can check for issues, so that we can solve any issues regarding player credentials.

Step 3: There will be number of challenges to unlock and solve. Next challenge will be unlocked only if first one is solved, and thereby completing all challenges and finding the treasure. Unlocked challenges appear in green colour.

Step 4: Download attached files with each challenge to find Supporting documents.

Step 4: Each challenge holds a certain point. Players are ranked in real time based on the scores. Tie breaks are automatically solved by time of completion of challenge.

Step 5: Real time statistics and scoreboard can be accessed from the menu bar.
